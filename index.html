Subscription isn't loading or shows an error?

This is completely normal for newly downloaded apps. Apple's servers need a few minutes to activate subscriptions. Close the app completely, wait a minute, then reopen it and try again. If it still doesn't work after an hour, email us.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → Tap your name at the top → Tap Subscriptions → Tap GLP Health Tracker → Tap Cancel Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

I was charged but Premium isn't working?

Open the app and tap "Start Premium" on any locked feature. Then tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the Premium screen. This reconnects your subscription. If it still doesn't work, email us with the Apple ID email you used to purchase.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are processed by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and select the subscription purchase.

How do I share my progress with my doctor?

Use the "Doctor Share" feature on the Home tab to generate a PDF summary of your treatment history. You can email, print, or AirDrop it to your healthcare provider. This feature is free for all users.

Is my health data private?

Yes. All your health data is stored locally on your device. We never see, access, or transmit your health information. See our Privacy Policy below for full details.

Can I recover my data if I delete the app?

No. All data is stored locally on your device — uninstalling the app permanently deletes your data. We recommend using Doctor Share to export a PDF backup before making any changes.

Is this app a medical device?

No. GLP Health Tracker is a personal wellness tracking tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your healthcare provider before making changes to your medication or treatment plan.
